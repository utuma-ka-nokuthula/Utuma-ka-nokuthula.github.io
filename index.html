About the Author

utuma-ka-nokuthula is an African writer, poet, and storyteller from Rwanda whose work explores the silent depths of the human heart.


Through poetry, reflective prose, and storytelling, he writes about love, faith, healing, gratitude, identity, loss, and personal growth. His words are shaped by both lived experience and careful observation of the emotional journeys people carry within themselves.


Through works such as Dead Feelings, The Bridge of Worlds, and Izimfano Zabakhokho, he invites readers into stories and reflections that challenge, comfort, and inspire.
